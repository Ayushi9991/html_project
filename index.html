Kurma

Vishnu’s **second avatar** was **Kurma (the Tortoise)**, who supported Mount Mandara during the **churning of the ocean (Samudra Manthan)**.

Varaha

Vishnu’s **third avatar** was **Varaha (the Boar)**, who rescued **Earth (Bhudevi)** from the demon **Hiranyaksha** and lifted it from the ocean.

Narasimha
